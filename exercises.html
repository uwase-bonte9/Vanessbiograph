Vanessa Mukansanga

1984-2021

"she taught me how a family should be .Not perfect ", but there for each other"

Biography

Vanessa was born in 1984 , was the second child in her family but she was like the first born of her family. She grew up in family of nine children , two boys and seven girls .From her childhood , she was responsible, coherent girl and a person you can talk to.In her every day life, she liked listening stories and cooking . She did her primary school in Base center , secondary school at Janja and APACE secondary School , university in ULK university .She did accounting in secondary and university but she did wedding decoration as her career . In her third year of university she lost her mother and she started being in charge of her sisters and brothers .She took care of them until the last day of her life. She got married in 2009 and she was mother of two children, one boy and one girl .During her parenting time, she liked cooking to her children especially during weekend , to talk to them whenever she was free and taught them to pray where every evening they had to share word of God by reading bible and worshiping . She was that mother who likes to see her children looking smart both physically and mentally . She was good mother to her children and friend to every one around her . From 2017 , her life started to change because of sickness and life challenges, it was difficult time to her and her family . In 2021 , she ended up seeing goodbye to life because of COVID-19 . She was treated in CHUB Hospital in Butare city and her little sister was the one who was taking care of her during her sickness but she did spend alot of time to the hospital , it took only five days .She died in night of 26/06/2021 at 2:00 am . She was big hearted , strong ,brave, loving and caring women .
